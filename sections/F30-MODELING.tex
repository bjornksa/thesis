\chapter{Mathematical Modeling and Methods}

Throughout this thesis, we will mainly be looking at (underwater) vehicle manipulator systems. These systems consist of a mobile base, together with one or several manipulator arms. The configuration of such systems can be described by the position and orientation of its base, along with the configuration of each of the manipulator joints.
